Since Day One

Our story

Landmark Landscaping Services was founded with a simple vision: to create beautiful, functional outdoor spaces that enhance the quality of life for our clients across Kenya.

From our humble beginnings in Nairobi, we've grown into one of the most trusted landscaping and irrigation companies in the region. Our success is built on a foundation of hard work, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to listening — really listening — to what each client wants.

Today, we continue to serve residential, commercial, and institutional clients, bringing creativity, craftsmanship, and sustainability to every project we undertake.
