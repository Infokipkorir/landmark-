    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I get a free quote?

    Contact us via phone, email, or fill out the contact form above. We'll schedule a consultation to assess your project and provide a detailed quote.

    What areas do you service?

    We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding regions, with extended services to major towns across Kenya including Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and more.

    How long does a landscaping project take?

    Project timelines vary based on scope. Small projects may take days while larger ones take weeks. We'll provide a timeline during the consultation phase.

    Do you offer maintenance services?

    Yes! We provide comprehensive maintenance packages to keep your landscape healthy and beautiful throughout the year. Ask us about our maintenance plans.

    Can you work with my budget?

    Absolutely. We work with clients of all budget levels and can suggest options and alternatives to meet your financial requirements.

    Are your materials sustainable?

    We prioritize sustainable practices and eco-friendly materials whenever possible, creating beautiful landscapes that benefit the environment.
