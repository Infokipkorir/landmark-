How a job actually runs

From first phone call to final walk-through

There's no mystery to it. This is the same process whether it's a small flower bed or a full compound.

1

We talk first

You tell us what's bugging you about the space, what you're hoping for, and roughly what you want to spend. No pressure, no jargon.

2

We come look

Someone walks the site with you — checks the soil, the slope, the sun, the drainage — the stuff that decides whether a plan will actually work.

3

We sketch the plan

You get a layout and a plant list built around your site and your budget, not a copy-paste template.

4

We get to work

Our crew handles the digging, planting, building, and wiring — and keeps you posted if anything changes along the way.

5

We tidy up

Final details, clean edges, a proper clean-up of the site — we don't call it done until it actually looks done.

6

We stay in touch

We'll tell you how to care for what's new, and we're a phone call away if a question comes up months later.
